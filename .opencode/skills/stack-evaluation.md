# Stack Evaluation

Propose exactly 2–3 ranked technology stack candidates. Do not make a final decision.

For each candidate provide:
- **Name**: stack identifier
- **Rationale**: why it fits the stated requirements
- **Trade-offs**: what it gives up or makes harder
- **Score** (1–10): weighted suitability score against the project requirements

Rank candidates from most to least suitable. Explain the ranking clearly.

Do NOT select a stack. Present options only. The final decision happens in Stage 2.
